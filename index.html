Helix's Recent Book

In My DNA

An action-packed superhero novel that follows the journey of a young protagonist who discovers that their extraordinary abilities are encoded in their very DNA. Born into a world where hidden powers lie dormant within a select few, the main character must navigate the challenges of growing up while learning to control their unique gifts. As they uncover secrets about their family's mysterious past, they are thrust into a conflict with a dangerous organization seeking to harness these genetic powers for sinister purposes.
